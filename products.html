PhiSense

DEMO video

About

Phisense is an interactive 3D BIM platform to provide factory engineers and owners with greater insight.

Phisense acts as a visualization hub where collected data from various sources is intuitively aggregated and presented in 3D to enhance our ability to infer more complex causal relationships pertaining to factory performance and overall operational requirements.

Usecase

Phisense is being used by drone service providers as a platform to provide 3D informational data. For example, in the case of telecom infrastructure inspection, Phisense is being used to associate inspected properties to each asset in 3D. Read more...

PhiScan

About

PhiSCAN is a 3D LiDAR scanning system developed by our team.Using PhiSCAN we can generate a 3D point cloud of a target in a matter of minutes.

Usecase

Engineers and Architects can use PhiScan can to create an accurate 3D map (point cloud) of the surround. The gerarated 3D map can be used to take an accurate measurements upto 2cm.

PhiScan can also be used to calculate the volume of a stokpile and generate elevationplan. Read more...

PhiNav

DEMO video

About

PhiNAV is a GPS-enabled, LiDAR-based pathfinding system. It utilizes a LiDAR scan of the target to figure out the optimum path to complete the objective.

Wind Turbine inspection is a routine task undertaken by the respective asset owner for maintenance. Nowadays drones are playing a key role in carrying out the WTG inspection.

With the increasing popularity of drone-based WTG inspection, the demand for autonomous inspection is rising.

To solve this problem we have developed this PhiNav product.

Purpose

Wind turbines are growing in popularity as the push for renewable energy is increasing. Read more...
