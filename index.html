About

PhiNAV is an autonomous drone navigation system.

It utilizes a set of sensors(hardware) to map out a path for the UAV to follow in order to carry out a mission.

Unlike traditional autonomous navigation software like, PX4 Autopilot, Q ground control etc,. PhiNAV allows user to create 3D flight plan across any rigid structure.

The 3D flight path allows user to perform complex flight like WTG blade inspection, flare stack inspection, construction site inspection, etc. .Read more...

PhiNAV

Problem Statement

  • In a world embracing green alternatives, wind energy is being extensively harnessed to meet the demand for reliable and renewable power.
  • In 2020, global installations of new wind power exceeded 90 GW, marking a remarkable 53% growth compared to 2019.
  • As the demand for wind turbines continues to rise, so does the need for regular inspections, creating an unprecedented and ever-increasing demand.
  • To keep up with this demand, the industry is rapidly adopting drones or unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) as a solution.

Challenges with drone based WTG inspection

  • Need for high skilled pilots: Most of the UAV based WTG inspections are being done manually and therefore One or multiple skilled pilots are needed for the job This increases the cost of logistics
  • Long down time: Performing manual data capture require 1 to 1.5 hrs of WTG downtime this is because the data collection is done one blade at a time For each balde the WTG needs to be rotated to a specific orientation

Application of PhiNAV

A UAV along with the PhiNAV system can be installed in a site permanently and using our flight planning software the UAV can be programmed to perform fully autonomous inspection at scheduled intervals

This system can be used at inspection of various static structures like:

  1. WTG Farm
  2. Solar Farm
  3. Factory Environment
  4. Chimneys
  5. Construction Facility

PhiSENSE

Problem Statement

  • It is challenging for factory management personnel to obtain a real-time overview of the processes taking place in the factory. The available process flow data usually only extends to the technician level, and any issues are often communicated to the management at a later stage.
  • We encountered a similar problem statement from telecom owners who faced difficulties in visualizing the current arrangement of telecom assets. As a result, they were unable to effectively plan and optimize the placement of incoming assets such as antennas and dishes.
  • To solve this problems, we created a web-based 3D Interactive platform called PhiSENSE. PhiSENSE serves as our backbone software, displaying data collected by our autonomous systems in an interactive dashboard.

Lightweight 3D Engine

  • Phisense is a powerful 3D engine built on webGL it can render 3D models onto the browser itself.

Advanced Interactivity

  • Click, drag and rotate 3D models Virtual walkthrough AR/VR compatible

IoT and DCS Integration

  • Phisense can be integrated with DCS and IoT for runtime data visualization and analytics

Fully Customizable

  • Through our APIs we give developer the freedom to customise the UI according to their needs

PhiSCAN

Problem Statement

  • Coromandel International is a leading Indian fertilizer manufacturer, producing an annual output of 4.5 million tonnes. To meet this demand, they process a substantial quantity of raw materials sourced from multiple vendors across various countries.
  • Raw materials procured by Coromandel are delivered to processing plants located in Visakhapatnam, Kakinada, and Ennore. At these plants, the raw materials are stored in godowns and utilized for production.
  • However, a challenge arises as each godown receives over 1000 kg of raw material weekly, and there is currently no process implemented to track the inventory of raw materials accurately.
  • CIL brought this problem statement to us. CIL had implemented a weighbridge to keep a track of incoming materials. But they are unable to track the consumption & realtime movement of the material.

PhiSCAN Market

  • For PoC and Product validation, we are deploying our solution in one of the warehouses of CIL. CIL is looking to install the solution in around 30 warehouses. We are also in talks with Indorama, which has around 40 such warehouses in India.
  • Our product can be used by customers aggregating construction materials for stockpile management.
  • It can be used for Mining material and ore management. Stockpile volumetry can be used to measure inventories of materials like iron ore and other minerals

Product Market

  • Any application regarding management of aggregates, ore, dry-bulk chemicals, or agricultural products. It can used in the management of various types of chemicals like - Petrochemicals, Agrochemicals, Food ingredient chemicals, etc.
  • Stockpiles at shipyards and ports require constant supervision and management. Since these particular stock yards are in direct conversation with shipping routes and global supply chains, the importance of accurate measurements and data cannot be overstated.

Problems in Stock Management

  • Traditional inventory tracking solutions lack precise location tracking in warehouses.
  • While these solutions provide information on item availability and quantity, they fail to pinpoint the exact location of items in real space. This becomes a real problem if you are managing multiple warehouses at different locations
  • This lack of real-time visibility and accurate tracking hampers operational efficiency and leads to challenges in storage and retrieval processes.
  • There is a need for advanced location tracking solutions that offer real-time tracking and optimization of item locations within the warehouse.
  • Realtime stock amount & tracking values can help with Optimized Space Utilization.

Solution

To solve the problem of precise location tracking, we propose an AI-based real-time inventory system.

  • The system utilizes computer vision and object recognition technologies.
  • It scans the warehouse and creates a 3D map of the surroundings.
  • Through this process, the system identifies the quantity of each item and its specific position within the warehouse.
  • This advanced approach enables accurate inventory tracking, providing real-time visibility of item locations.

Product Overview

We propose following solution

  1. A fully autonomous rover based solution: A robotic camera/scanner system mounted on a rail track, build inside warehouse, to move autonomously at predefined intervals and scan the boxes or items present in the warehouse.
  2. A semi autonomous handheld 3D scanner: A handheld 3D scanner to be used by warehouse personnel to move inside the warehouse and scan the items manually at required intervals
  3. The scanner is a custom hardware, which consists of various sensors like camera, barcode scanner, indoor positioning system etc.
  4. Once the items are scanned, the scanner uses sensors to create a 3D map of the environment to show the exact locations of the items & show any required items.
